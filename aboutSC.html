About Sandra Cisneros

Sandra Cisneros is a Chicana poet and writer who was born in 1954 in Chicago, Illinois. In addition to her poetry, she is the author of acclaimed fiction including The House on Mango Street. Cisneros is the recipient of a MacArthur Fellowship and the PEN/Nabokov Award for International Literature. She is also the founder of the Macando Writers Workshop.

Cisneros has served as an educator for high school students and university students, in addition to her roles as a university administrator and recruiter. She is a graduate of the Iowa Writers' Workshop and currently resides in central Mexico.
